## ЁЯУМ Project Overview

The EV Adoption Analysis Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyze electric vehicle adoption trends, customer preferences, vehicle performance, and environmental impact between 2020 and 2024.

The dashboard provides insights into EV sales growth, adoption drivers, brand performance, country-wise adoption, customer satisfaction, and COтВВ savings through a visually appealing and interactive interface.

## ЁЯФН Key Insights

- EV adoption peaked in 2023.
- Battery Electric Vehicles (BEVs) account for the largest share of adoption.
- Environmental concerns are the primary driver of EV purchases.
- Tesla offers the highest average driving range among all brands.
- China and the United States lead EV adoption.
- Electric vehicle adoption contributes approximately **21 Million KG** of COтВВ savings.
- Customer satisfaction remains high at **4.0/5**.
